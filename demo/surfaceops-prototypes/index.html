SurfaceOps prototype review

Three evidence-first review prototypes

Each prototype explores a different working shape for SurfaceOps. They are static review artifacts guided by JudgmentKit activity criteria and the repo's SurfaceOps UI criteria.

Boundary: these prototypes do not implement live SurfaceOps, live JudgmentKit, persistent review storage, production adapters, APIs, SDKs, A2UI, or work-order execution. Approval means evidence eligibility for a later authorized consumer.
Prototype A

Evidence Workbench

A dense three-pane console for reviewers who need queue scanning, evidence inspection, and decision controls in one view.

  • Queue rail with status filters.
  • Evidence and diagnostics beside item detail.
  • State-gated decision panel.

Decision Flow

Governance Board

A board-shaped review surface that keeps cards lightweight while evidence and decisions stay in a dedicated panel.
